Meet our teacher

Hello welcome to NES. My name is Neil and I am from Ireland. I am a native English speaker with a vast experience of teaching both kids and adults in Japan. I am also a professionally trained teacher and hold a Masters Degree in Teaching English as a foreign language. I opened NES in 2024 to help students in Wakayama to fulfill their dreams by becoming fluent speakers of English. My hobbies are cycling, I usually cycle over 100 km a week and going to the cinema to watch movies. I also love travelling and have visited all 47 Prefectures of Japan. Im looking forward to meeting you at my classroom soon.

The Advantages of Having a Native Instructor

Correct Pronunciation: Native speakers can show you how words are really spoken and help you sound more like a native when you talk.

Natural Language Use: They can teach you how English is used in everyday life, so you can understand and use it better.

Cultural Insights: Native speakers can also share information about their culture, which helps you understand the language in a cultural context.

Experienced

Our teacher has more than 13 years of experience teaching English in Japan. He has worked as a teacher at a leading British English language school and as an ALT (Assistant Language Teacher) at elementary and junior high schools in Tokyo, Osaka, and Wakayama. He has worked for an industry leader, Eiken focused English school for elementary kids. He has experience teaching Eiken, IELTS, TOEIC, and TOEFL. He has taught business lessons in-house at some of Japan’s leading companies, government agencies, and the Japanese Self-Defence Forces. He can speak Japanese to a near-native level (JLPT Level 2). He has a deep understanding of Japanese culture and can enhance language learning by bridging cultural and linguistic gaps. We provide personalised lessons that help students overcome common challenges Japanese learners of English face.

Professional

Our teacher is a professionally qualified English teacher. He completed a one month course in TEFL (teaching English as a foreign language). He also completed a Masters Degree in TESOL (Teaching English to Students of Other Languages) at Ireland’s leading university, Trinity College Dublin.
Our teacher has advanced knowledge of language acquisition theories, teaching methodology, and linguistics and an in-depth understanding of the English language to help students grasp complex grammar, improve vocabulary, and develop proficiency.


Graded Classes

We don’t arrange classes by age or school grade; we use students experience, ability, and proficiency (using Eiken level) to create tailored classes at the appropriate level for each student. All students parents must complete our counseling sheet which will help our teacher to choose the appropriate level for their child’s trial lesson (taiken). All students must take a trial lesson before they are assigned a class.

What We Offer

Eiken Support

Our teacher has extensive experience teaching Eiken to elementary school children. In my experience of teaching Eiken students have no problems passing the Grade 5 test once they do some self study at home and so we don’t offer specialised classes for Grade 5. I will be happy to give advice to any students who apply for the Grade 5 test.
From Eiken Grade 4, we offer separate specialized classes in Eiken. We will explain study a vocabulary list of commonly used words, I will explain how to answer Eiken questions, how to analyse reading passages, and for Grade 3 and upwards how to write essays and how to pass the interview test. We will offer Eiken classes for Grade 4, 3, Pre 2 and 2. Students who wish to improve their overall English skills, to become fluent speakers and who also want to pass the Eiken can take both our regular classes and our dedicated Eiken classes back to back as a 110 minute weekly lesson.

Adult Lessons

Private lessons are offered for Adults. All students must complete a trial lesson so they can be assigned an appropriate textbook for their level. Classes can be booked to let students choose a suitable time for them. Adult lessons cost 4000/50 minute lessons. Students can decide what they want to study – conversational English, travel English, business English or exam English (TOEIC, Eiken, IELTS etc.)

Kids Lessons

All kids lessons are 50 minutes and costs 7000 yen per month (42 lessons per year). There is also an additional charge for textbooks (prices vary by class) and an administration and insurance charge of 5000 yen per year. The maximum number of students in a group is 8.
Classes are available from kindergarten nen chou to the 6th grade of elementary. Private lessons for junior high school and high school aged students can be booked and costs 4000/lesson.

Kids Group Lessons

  • First Steps (Young Learners up to 3rd Grade, no English experience)
  • Kids 1 (Young Learners with some English experience)
  • Kids 2 (Learners with more than 1 year experience)
  • Kids 3 (Learners with 2 years experience, passed Eiken 5)
  • Kids 4 (Learners with 3 years experience, passed Eiken 4)
  • Kids 5 (Learners with +3 years experience, passed Eiken 3)
  • Kids Advanced (Learners with extensive experience, passed Eiken Pre 2)
